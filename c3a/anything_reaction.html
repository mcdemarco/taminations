Anything Reaction

Starting formations - depends on the "Anything" call

Do the anything call leaving off the final Extend, then do a full Chain Reaction.

The anything call is usually a Scoot Back variation of a Tagging call. For example,

Flip Reaction: From Parallel Waves. Flip Back Centers To A Wave then all Chain Reaction.

Tag Reaction: From Parallel Lines. Tag Back To A Wave Centers To A Wave then all Chain Reaction.

Note: For Tagging calls, there is an unspoken 'Back' (Scoot Back) in them (e.g., Tag Reaction means Tag Back Reaction).
