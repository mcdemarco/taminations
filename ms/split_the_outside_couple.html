Couple #1 Split Couple #3, Separate and go around 3, meet partner at home and Swing

Dance action The active or directed dancers move forward between the two inactive dancers (splitting them). The inactive dancers move apart to let them through then move back together. Ending formation (before the next command): Active dancers with their backs to the dancers they split. Inactive dancers end where they started.
